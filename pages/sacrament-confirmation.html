Sacrament of Initiation

Confirmation

"You will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes upon you." Acts 1:8

Confirmation completes the grace of Baptism, sealing us with the gifts of the Holy Spirit for an active life in Christ and His Church.

About this Sacrament

Confirmation completes the grace of Baptism by a special outpouring of the Gifts of the Holy Spirit, which seal or "confirm" the baptized in union with Christ and empower them for active participation in the Church.

The goal of St. Peter's Confirmation program is to assist young people in their preparation. Because candidates are being confirmed into a faith community, they must belong to and worship with this community. The candidate's sponsor plays a major role and is expected to attend specific events.

Those preparing for Confirmation are required to fully participate in Perissos (Wednesday nights).
