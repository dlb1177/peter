Decorations & Cemetery Guidelines

Decorations

Families are welcome to place flowers, plants, and other decorations on graves as a way to honor their loved ones. We ask that decorations be maintained and removed periodically, particularly before ground maintenance work. Fresh flowers are preferred and should be removed after they have wilted. Seasonal decorations are welcome for holidays and special occasions.

Memorial Plantings

Shrubs, trees, and other plantings in memory of loved ones require approval from the cemetery office. This ensures proper maintenance and prevents overcrowding. Contact Peter Guzulaitis to discuss approved planting options.

Ground Care & Maintenance

The cemetery grounds are maintained by the parish to preserve the peaceful, well-kept appearance of the cemetery. Families should not place structures, fencing, or other installations on graves without prior approval.

Visiting Hours

The cemetery is open during daylight hours throughout the year. Families are invited to visit to remember and honor their loved ones. For winter visits, please be aware that grounds may be snow-covered and roads may not be plowed.

Planning Ahead

Many families find it helpful to make cemetery arrangements in advance. We encourage you to contact Peter Guzulaitis to discuss burial options, plot selection, and other arrangements at your own pace. Pre-planning can provide peace of mind for you and your family.
