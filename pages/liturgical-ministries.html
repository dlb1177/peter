Liturgical Ministries

"Each one should use whatever gift he has received to serve others." 1 Peter 4:10

The Mass is the work of the whole Body of Christ, and many hands are needed to help it unfold beautifully and prayerfully. Whatever your gifts, there is a place for you to serve at the altar of the Lord — and no experience is required to begin.

Lectors

Proclaim the Word of God at Mass, helping the assembly hear the Scriptures with clarity and reverence.

Eucharistic Ministers

Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion assist in distributing the Body and Blood of Christ at Mass and to the homebound.

Altar Servers

Young people and adults assist the priest at the altar, serving close to the heart of the Mass. A wonderful ministry for our youth.

Ushers & Greeters

Welcome everyone at the door, help seat the assembly, take up the collection, and make every visitor feel at home.

Sacristans

Prepare the sacred vessels, vestments, and worship space before Mass so that everything is ready for prayer.

Music Ministry

Cantors, choir members, and instrumentalists lead the assembly in sung prayer and lift our worship in song.

Ready to Serve?

We would love to help you find the ministry that fits your gifts and your schedule. Committed ministers also use our online ministry scheduler to view and manage their assignments. To get started or to learn more, reach out and we'll connect you with the right ministry leader.
