Instructions for Making Securities Gifts

If you are planning on gifting stocks, bonds, or other securities to our church, there are special instructions to be followed listed below.

If individual certificates are in your possession, please submit the following directly to St. Peter's transfer agent:

  • The Certificate
  • The Stock Power*
  • The Third-Party Release*

The back of the certificate and Third-Party Releases need to be signature guaranteed. LPL does not provide signature guarantees. The donor will be required to obtain this guarantee at their own financial institution.
